Validating a protocol

The business protocols editor allows to potentially design protocols that are not well-formed. For example, one protocol could be split into two disconnected graphs, or a non-final state could have no outgoing transitions. The goal of the business protocols validation plug-in is to detect such problems and report them to the user.

There are three different types of checkings. They can either be run individually or collectively, by using the buttons in the view and / or double-clicking on the checkings names in the list. Another possibility is to use the contextual menu entry that is available from the active project view. In this case, the three different checkings will be run.
